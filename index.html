Linguistic Analysis · The Excuse

The Lame Man · John 5:7

"Sir, I have no one to help me into the pool. Someone else goes down ahead of me."

LINGUISTIC FOCUS

"No one" → Absence of support

"Someone else" → External blame

Zero mention of his own desire

Teacher Prompt

"He never says 'I want to.' He only speaks of what is missing. When God asks you to move — do you lead with your desire, or with your deficit?"
